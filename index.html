About Me

My name is Santiago Fernandez, I am a second generation immigrant, my parents moved here from Colombia in their thirties when my older brother was 7 years old and my mother was pregnant with me. My younger brother was born a year and a half later and we have all been inseperable ever since! I love spending time with friends and family, especially when trying new activities or visiting new places. I grew up playing all kinds of sports and playing different instruments. Nowadays I enjoy playing guitar, cooking, and exercising. I recently graduated from Wilfrid Laurier University where I majored in Computer Science and minored in Film.
